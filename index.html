This is a website dedicated to the sport of Padel. Here you can learn more about the game, the rules, and find Padel clubs and facilities in your area.

What is Padel?

Padel is a racquet sport that combines elements of tennis, squash, and badminton. It is played in doubles on a smaller court surrounded by walls. The rules are similar to tennis, but the walls can be used as part of the game, adding a new dimension of strategy and skill.

Learn More

Padel Rules

Padel has many rules that are similar to tennis. The main differences are that the ball can be played off the walls, the serve must be made underhand, and there are specific rules for where players must stand during the serve and return.
